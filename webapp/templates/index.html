NACP-2 WOMEN AND MINORITIES IN THE WORKFORCE

This project allows you to sift through employment statistics collected from the US Bureau of Labor. We seek to make apparent dissimilarities in employment based on gender and race identity in the US, across a variety of government recognized industrial sectors.


Click on either industries or identities to begin.
